FAQs

What is Legalix?

▼

Legalix is a legal tech platform that connects people with lawyers and provides legal information and resources.



How do I find a lawyer on Legalix?

▼

You can search for lawyers based on your legal issue and location. Use the search bar on the homepage to get started.



Is Legalix a free service?

▼

Legalix offers both free and paid services. Some features are available for free, while others may require payment.
